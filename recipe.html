Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A rich and hearty Italian pasta dish, perfect for family dinners. This recipe combines tender minced beef, a savory tomato sauce, and aromatic herbs to create a flavor-packed meal everyone will love.

Original inspiration from BBC Good Food

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty Level: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g minced beef
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic; saut√© until soft and fragrant.
  3. Stir in minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and basil; mix well.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  7. Drain spaghetti and serve topped with the bolognese sauce.

Tips

For a richer flavor, let the sauce simmer longer. You can also add a splash of red wine for depth. Garnish with fresh parsley and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese before serving.
